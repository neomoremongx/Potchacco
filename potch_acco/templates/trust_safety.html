Trust & safety

Your peace of mind is our foundation. We build PotchAcco so you can focus on finding the right place – safely.

PotchAcco is a Stacguru initiative. We don't handle payments or contracts – we simply connect you directly with verified local providers. Trust is built on transparency, vetting, and clear communication.

Provider vetting
Every landlord, guesthouse or residence manager is manually verified by our team. We confirm identity, physical presence in Potch, and basic reliability.
Direct contact
We never intermediate your conversations. You call, WhatsApp or email the provider directly – no hidden forms, no data mining.
Real photos, real people
Profile photos and cover images are uploaded by the provider. We encourage authentic imagery – what you see is what you get.
Community ratings
Ratings come from real tenants. They help you gauge reliability before reaching out.

How we verify providers

Every provider listed on PotchAcco goes through a manual check by the Stacguru team. We don't rely on algorithms.

  • Business/ID check: we ask for a company registration or ID to confirm identity.
  • Location proof: provider must demonstrate they operate in Potchefstroom (utility bill, lease, or site visit).
  • Active contact: we test phone and WhatsApp numbers before activation.
  • Ongoing monitoring: if users report unresponsive or inaccurate listings, we re-verify or remove the provider.

Staying safe while renting

While we vet providers, it's important to stay alert when making any rental agreement. Here are our tips:

  • Always view the property first – never pay a deposit without seeing the room or house.
  • Use the contact details on the card – if someone contacts you via a different number, it might be a scam.
  • Get a written lease – even for short stays, a simple agreement protects both sides.
  • Never pay via untraceable methods – prefer bank transfers with reference, not cash or gift cards.

What we expect from providers

Being listed on PotchAcco comes with a commitment to honesty and responsiveness.

  • Respond promptly: aim to answer calls or WhatsApp messages within 24h.
  • Keep photos accurate: update cover/profile images if the property changes significantly.
  • No discrimination: we welcome everyone – providers must comply with South Africa's equality laws.
  • Report unavailability: if units are full, let us know so we can update your listing.

Report a concern

If you encounter a listing that seems fake, a provider who is unresponsive, or any suspicious behaviour, tell us immediately. We take every report seriously.

Beware of scams: PotchAcco will never ask you for money, banking details, or payments via the site. We only display contact info. If anyone impersonating PotchAcco contacts you, report it immediately.
