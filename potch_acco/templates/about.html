Why PotchAcco exists

The team at Stacguru — a Potchefstroom‑based IT business — kept hearing the same frustration from friends, students and travellers: “Finding a decent place to rent is exhausting, and you never know if the landlord is reliable.” At the same time, great guesthouses and residence managers struggled to get in front of the right people without expensive ads.

That’s when Stacguru decided to apply its digital problem‑solving mindset to the local accommodation gap. They built PotchAcco as a clean, direct showcase: no algorithms, no noise — just vetted providers with real contact details, WhatsApp numbers, and actual photos.

“We're tech people, but this problem wasn't about fancy AI — it was about transparency. We built the tool we wished existed when we were house hunting.”

Today PotchAcco is run as a dedicated platform under the Stacguru umbrella, but the ethos remains the same: use simple digital tools to solve a real, local problem. Every provider is still manually verified, and the site stays ad‑free and clutter‑free.
